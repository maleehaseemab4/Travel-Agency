About Us

Welcome to Toury Travel Agency

Toury Travel Agency was founded in 2025 with a vision to make Umrah and Hajj journeys easier and safer for Muslims worldwide. We are dedicated to connecting millions of Muslims throughout the world to visit the most peaceful places on earth and create memories that will last a lifetime.

Our team of experienced travel consultants specializes in crafting personalized pilgrimages that cater to your specific needs and preferences. With our deep knowledge of sacred sites and local customs, we ensure that your spiritual journey is both meaningful and comfortable.

Our Mission

At Toury, our mission is to facilitate transformative spiritual journeys by providing exceptional travel services that prioritize safety, comfort, and authentic experiences. We strive to make sacred pilgrimages accessible to all Muslims, regardless of their background or experience with international travel.

Why Choose Us

With Toury, you gain access to:

• Expert guides familiar with all aspects of Umrah and Hajj rituals
• Carefully selected accommodations close to holy sites
• 24/7 support throughout your journey
• Competitive pricing with transparent policies
• Personalized itineraries that respect your pace and preferences

PRIVACY POLICY

At Toury, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Passport details (for booking international travel)
  • Payment details
  • Travel preferences and history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process bookings and payments
  • Send booking confirmations and travel updates
  • Respond to your inquiries and customer support requests
  • Improve our services and personalize your experience
  • Send marketing emails (only with your permission)

3. Data Protection

We use encryption and secure servers to protect your personal information. Only authorized employees have access to your data.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Airlines, hotels, and tour operators (only as needed for your booking)
  • Legal authorities if required by law

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve user experience. You can manage cookie settings through your browser at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
